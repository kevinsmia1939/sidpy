=====
sidpy
=====

**Python utilities for storing, visualizing, and processing Spectroscopic and Imaging Data (SID)**

This package supports other scientific packages such as:

* `pyNSID <../pyNSID/about.html>`_
* `pyUSID <../pyUSID/about.html>`_
* `pycroscopy <../pycroscopy/about.html>`_

Please the side panel on the left for other documentation pages on ``sidpy``
